Why CSIS?

Computer Science and Information Systems (CSIS) is one of the most popular departments on campus. Why? 

  • Fun. If you like to tinker with your computer's software, then CSIS is about the most fun you can have. Learning on your own is possible, but we have found that learning together is more effective, faster, and more fun!
  • Interesting. Many CSIS classes involve computer programming. The mental challenge is curiously engaging, and continued practice builds mind-bending logical reasoning skills.
  • Economically stable. All major local employers and several major regional employers actively recruit CSIS current students for internships and graduates for full time employment. Nationwide starting salaries average $59,800.
